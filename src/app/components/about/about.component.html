About Me

My name is Yassine Nachate, and I am a passionate Full Stack Engineer with expertise in web and mobile development. I hold a degree in Information Systems Engineering from the Institut Supérieur des Ingénieurs et des Affaires (ISGA) in Marrakech, specializing in software development.

Over the years, I have worked on several innovative projects including web applications for event management, e-commerce platforms, and fleet management systems. My work combines technical expertise with creativity, ensuring that solutions are efficient, scalable, and user-friendly.

I am always looking for opportunities to learn and grow. With a strong foundation in technologies like React.js, Symfony, Angular, and Spring Boot, I aim to contribute to projects that have a real impact while continuing to enhance my skills.
